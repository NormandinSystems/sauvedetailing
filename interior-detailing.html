The Ultimate Reset for Your Car's Interior

Montreal winters can be brutal on your vehicle's interior. Between the calcium and salt stains dragged in on your boots, the inevitable coffee spills during morning commutes, and the buildup of dust and allergens, regular car washes simply aren't enough. Our premium mobile interior car cleaning service is designed to deeply extract dirt from your carpets, seats, and hard-to-reach crevices.

Whether you're dealing with stubborn pet hair, lingering smoke odors, or seats that have lost their original luster, Sauve Detailing brings professional-grade equipment directly to your home or office. We don't just wipe down surfaces; we sanitize, condition, and protect your entire cabin.

Our Signature Interior Detailing Process

1

Deep Vacuum & Blowout

We start by using compressed air to blow out debris from tight cracks, under seats, and air vents. This is followed by a rigorous, thorough vacuuming of the entire interior, including the trunk.

2

Hot Water Extraction & Shampoo

We apply specialized fabric cleaners to seats and floor mats, agitating the material to lift embedded grime. Our hot water extractor then acts as a heavy-duty carpet shampooer, pulling out years of dirt and salt stains.

3

Steam Cleaning & Sanitization

Using high-temperature steam, we melt away sticky residue in cup holders, disinfect steering wheels, and clean door panels without the use of harsh, sticky chemicals.

4

Leather & Plastic Conditioning

If your car has leather seats, we gently clean and condition them to prevent cracking and fading. All interior plastics and vinyls receive a UV-protectant dressing that leaves a matte, non-greasy factory finish.

Why Invest in Professional Interior Cleaning?

Health & Hygiene

Car interiors trap millions of microscopic allergens, bacteria, and mold spores. Deep steam cleaning eliminates these threats, making your daily commute healthier.

Maximized Resale Value

A clean, odor-free interior is the #1 factor buyers notice. A professional detail can easily add hundreds of dollars to your car's trade-in value.
