Defend Your Clear Coat with a Professional Detail

The roads of the Greater Montreal Area are incredibly demanding on your vehicle's paint. From the highly abrasive salt, sand, and calcium mixtures used during our harsh winters to the baking UV rays of summer, your car's clear coat is constantly under attack. Taking your car through an automatic drive-thru wash might seem convenient, but their stiff brushes act like sandpaper, leaving thousands of micro-scratches and swirl marks on your finish.

At Sauve Detailing, we offer a 100% scratch-free mobile exterior detailing service. We utilize the industry-standard two-bucket hand wash method, pH-neutral snow foams, and ultra-plush microfiber towels. By meticulously decontaminating the paint and sealing it with a premium hydrophobic wax, we restore that slick, mirror-like gloss while providing months of durable protection.

Our 5-Step Exterior Detail Process

1

Wheels, Tires & Fender Wells

We always start with the dirtiest part of the car. We use specialized non-acidic wheel cleaners and an assortment of soft brushes to safely break down heavy brake dust, road grime, and mud inside the wheel barrels and tire walls.

2

Snow Foam Pre-Wash

Before we ever touch the paint, we blanket the vehicle in a thick layer of pH-neutral snow foam. This dwells on the surface, encapsulating loose dirt and safely lifting it off the clear coat to prevent washing-induced swirl marks.

3

Two-Bucket Hand Wash

Using premium microfiber wash mitts, we perform a gentle hand wash. We use a two-bucket system (one for soapy water, one for rinsing the mitt) with grit guards to ensure we never rub dirt back into your paint.

4

Paint Decontamination (Optional Clay Bar)

We chemically remove iron particles and embedded tar. For a perfectly smooth finish, we recommend adding our clay bar treatment, which pulls out microscopic contaminants stuck in the pores of the clear coat.

5

Hydrophobic Wax Application

After carefully drying the vehicle with forced air and plush towels, we apply a premium synthetic wax or sealant. This creates a deep, wet-looking gloss and leaves behind a hydrophobic layer that repels water and dirt for up to 3 months.

Benefits of Routine Exterior Detailing

Prevent Rust & Degradation

Removing caustic road salt and bug splatter prevents these acidic materials from eating through your clear coat and causing premature rust spots on your vehicle's body.

Easier Maintenance

A waxed car is incredibly easy to clean. Dirt and grime struggle to stick to the slick, hydrophobic surface, meaning your car stays looking cleaner for much longer.
